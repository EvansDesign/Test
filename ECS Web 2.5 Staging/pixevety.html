Pixevety - Multi-platform App

Pixevety is an image management solution designed for schools and other family-focused organizations–and their communities–to deliver a private, central online platform to collect, manage, protect and share high-quality photos and other media assets.

Working closely with product managers and development teams, Evans Creative helped evaluate early product builds providing UX feedback and suggesting improvements. UI design and specifications were executed based on ongoing consultation with Pixevety, as well as providing marketing teams with visual design and creative assistance as needed.

  • Client: Pixevety
  • User Experience: Ted Evans
  • User Interface Design: Ted Evans
  • Design & Marketing Support: Ted Evans
