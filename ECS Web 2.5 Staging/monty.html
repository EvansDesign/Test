Monty Python - Live Merchandising Presentation

This was the opening of a live merchandising presentation at the Groucho Club in London. It was developed in Macromedia Director so that the live presenter could cue through animated slides, presenting the latest merchandise being developed.

We were given some of Terry Gilliam's original hand drawn cut-outs to use as the basis for our all digital animation, as well as audio and music. This was a lot of fun to work on and we were privileged to meet three of the Pythons during production.

  • Client: The Partnership / Python (Monty) Pictures
  • Creative Direction: Ted Evans
  • Animation: Ted Evans
  • Artwork: Terry Gilliam
